For Dealers

Why Did You Start Great Coins?

Simple, we wanted to create a market where trusted dealers could meet collectors without anyone getting in the way.

How do you help Dealers?

What Do Dealers Need Help with Most?

  • Marketing
  • Finding Customers
  • Reducing my cost to Market and Find New Customers :)

The current Bay allows you great access to collectors, it only asks for ~50% of your gross margins. Great Coins was founded by a dealer and collector who wanted the industry to regain control and not let the dollars leave the Collectible Market.

Can I change My Plan At Any Time?

Yes, simply go to your Account tab in the Shop admin to change plans at any time.

Do you Charge any Listing Fees?

{{marketplace.title|title}} does not charge for:

  • Insertion
  • Final Value
  • Reserve
  • For Sale
  • Auction
  • Picture Hosting

No, The only limitations in your account are noted, the total items you can list a month, the total storage space and annual Store Revenue

Do I have to sign a long term contract?

No. {{marketplace.title|title}} is a montly subscription with payment only for what you use. We do not require long-term contracts, you only pay month-to-month. If you want to cancel, you will be billed for the current month, but you won't be billed again.
